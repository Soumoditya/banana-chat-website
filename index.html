Why Banana?

Not just another chat app.
A complete social platform.

Messaging, Evolved

DMs, groups, broadcasts, swipe-to-reply, reactions, voice notes, file sharing, GIFs — every messaging feature you've ever wanted, in one app.

Make It Yours

Custom themes, fonts, UI skins, app icons, iOS emojis — 4 premium tiers that let you express yourself beyond just a profile pic.

Privacy First

Private profiles, close friends lists, bidirectional blocking, Firestore security rules, guest mode. Your data stays yours.

Features

Everything you'd expect.
And a lot you wouldn't.

Real-Time Messaging

DMs, group chats, and broadcast channels. Swipe-to-reply, emoji reactions, pinned messages, read receipts, typing indicators, and delivered ticks.

FirestoreReal-timeEncrypted

Stories & Highlights

24h stories with custom backgrounds, fonts, and text styling. Archive them. Pin them as highlights on your profile.

Voice Notes

Waveform audio player with visual playback. Record, send, listen — with duration tracking and inline controls.

Social Feed

Multi-media posts with upvote/downvote, threaded comments, file attachments, reshares, @mentions, and poll support.

MediaVotingCommentsPolls

Voice & Video Calls

One-tap calling with dedicated call UI. Integrated right into chat threads — no switching apps.

Streaks & Engagement

Snapchat-style streaks powered by Firebase RTDB. Daily counters, fire badges. Keep chatting or lose your streak.

Smart Notifications

Push + local notifications with foreground alerts, background delivery, and deep-link tap handling via Expo.

Admin Dashboard

Full admin panel with user management, premium approvals, verification, ban/unban, broadcasts, and live analytics.

ModerationAnalyticsBroadcast

Trash & Recovery

30-day soft-delete with one-tap restoration. Recover anything — posts, stories, or archived content.

Social Graph

Friends, followers, close friends, blocking with full bidirectional cleanup. Private profiles and audience controls.

File & Doc Sharing

Send any file type — PDFs, ZIPs, docs. Full metadata display with file size, name, and one-tap download.

Media Viewer

Full-screen image viewer, video player with native controls, GIF inline rendering, and gallery downloads.
